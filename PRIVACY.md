# Privacy Policy — Open Tab to the Right

_Last updated: 2025_

## Summary

**Open Tab to the Right does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

## What the extension does

The extension performs two functions entirely on your local device:

1. Moves newly created tabs to the right of the current tab.
2. Optionally focuses the tab on the left when you close the active tab.

To do this it reads the position (index) of your tabs within a window and
moves or focuses them. This processing happens locally in your browser and is
never sent anywhere.

## Permissions

- **`tabs`** — Required to read tab positions and to move/focus tabs. The
  extension does not read page contents, URLs are only used internally to
  identify tabs, and no tab information leaves your device.
- **`storage`** — Used only to remember your on/off preference for the
  "focus left on close" feature. This setting is stored via Chrome's
  `storage.sync` so it can follow your Chrome profile. It contains a single
  boolean value and no personal information.

## Data collection

- We do **not** collect analytics.
- We do **not** use cookies or trackers.
- We do **not** send any data to remote servers.
- We do **not** sell or share any data with third parties.
