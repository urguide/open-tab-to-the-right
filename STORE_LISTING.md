## Name

Open Tab to the Right

## Short / summary

Always open new tabs to the right of the current tab, and optionally focus the
left tab when you close one.

## Category

Productivity

## Detailed description (English)

Tired of new tabs jumping to the far right of your tab bar, far away from what
you were doing? Open Tab to the Right keeps your browsing in order.

Features:

- New tab on the right — Every new tab opens immediately to the right of the
  tab you are currently on, whether you press Ctrl/Cmd+T or open a link in a
  new tab.

- Focus left on close (optional) — When you close the active tab, focus moves
  to the tab on its left instead of the right. Toggle this on or off from the
  toolbar popup.

Privacy first:

- No accounts, no tracking, no ads.
- Nothing about your tabs ever leaves your device.
- Open source.

Permissions explained:

- "Read and change your tabs" (tabs): needed to read tab positions and to
  move/focus tabs. Page contents are never read.
- "storage": stores a single on/off preference for the focus-left feature.

## Single purpose statement (required by Google)

This extension has a single purpose: to control the placement and focus of
browser tabs — opening new tabs to the right of the active tab and optionally
focusing the left tab when the active tab is closed.

## Permission justifications (paste into dashboard)

- **tabs**: Required to read the index/position of tabs and to move newly
  created tabs and change the focused tab. No page content is accessed.
- **storage**: Required to persist the user's single on/off preference for the
  "focus left on close" feature.
